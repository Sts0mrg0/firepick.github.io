FirePick SMD Pick-and-Place Machine Kits

Build your own FirePick SMD pick-and-place (PnP) machine!

FireSight pick and place machine kits are:

  • open source. Designs are provided under the MIT license.
  • low cost. Entry-level FirePick designs for hobbyists are <USD 499.
  • easy to assemble. FirePick kits only require hand tools. Power tools save you time but aren't required.
  • open supply chain. Purchase your parts directly from approved vendors and reduce middleman costs.
  • verified. Each FirePick finalized design has been verified from purchase through assembly and tested with documented specifications.
